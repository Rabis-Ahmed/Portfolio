About me

I’m a Website Developer with years of experience specializing in WordPress. I can convert designs from PSD, XD, or Figma into responsive WordPress sites using Bootstrap. I’m skilled in Custom Post Types and well-versed in popular page builders like Kadence Blocks, Elementor, Divi, WP Bakery, and more.
